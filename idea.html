Business Idea Summary

Business Idea

We enable restaurants to monetize table space through partnerships with brands. Ads will include QR codes, short links, promo codes, and NFC where needed to measure engagement and conversions.

The model is scalable across cities and restaurant formats — from local cafés to chains — and focuses on low-cost, high-attention ad placements.
