About TableDineX

TableDineX is an advertising platform converting restaurant tables into brand engagement hubs. Our goal is to bridge brands and customers in a creative, measurable, and cost-effective way.

We provide physical ad placements (table tents, placemats, coasters, NFC tags) combined with a lightweight SaaS portal for campaign management and analytics.
